06 — Motion & texture

How it moves & feels

Motion is calm and earns its place: content settles in as you scroll, hovers give a gentle lift, and a fine grain plus soft teal light keep surfaces tactile rather than flat. All of it switches off for visitors who prefer reduced motion.

Scroll reveals

Sections rise 22px and fade in once, on a 700ms ease-out curve. Quiet and deliberate — never bouncy or springy.

Hover & load

Cards lift 4px, link arrows nudge, buttons deepen to Deep Teal, and the hero settles in on load — all around 160–800ms.

Texture

A ≤5% film grain over the whole page, a slow teal glow drifting behind the hero, and brass kept to hairlines only.
