04 — Texture & imagery

The feel

Built from the palette — calm surfaces, one brass line, soft teal light.

Teal gradient
Paper / fine grain
Charcoal + brass line
Soft teal light

Photography. Real workplaces and unposed moments — people mid-conversation, natural light, generous negative space. Calm and observational, never staged stock energy.

Grade. Muted and slightly desaturated with a faint teal cast in the shadows. Avoid saturated, high-contrast, "bright young startup" colour.
