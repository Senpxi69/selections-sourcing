Our Journey So Far

We Build Lasting Relationships Between
Candidates & Businesses.

Established in 2014, Selections Sourcing has been providing end-to-end Human Resource solutions to organisations — right from recruitment to background verification. We are based out of Thane, Maharashtra, India.

Some of the most knowledgeable, skilled, passionate and seasoned search experts are part of our young and proficient team of HR consultants. The new-age, evolving and dynamic needs of both start-ups and established companies are supported by our objective-focused solutions.

Our proactive team of expert head-hunters, backed by state-of-the-art technology recruitment solutions, makes us a bankable source of opportunity and talent in today's global Human Resource marketplace.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To connect exceptional people with organisations where they can thrive — through honest, specialist recruitment that respects the time and ambitions of everyone involved. We measure success not by placements made, but by careers and businesses we help grow.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted HR sourcing partner — known for quality over volume, relationships over transactions, and a reputation that means companies and candidates come back to us again and again throughout their journeys.

How We Work

The Values Behind
Every Placement

These principles guide every brief we take on and every candidate we represent.

Integrity First

We give honest advice — even when it's not what you want to hear. No inflated CVs, no overselling roles. Trust is everything.

Quality Over Volume

We'd rather send you three perfect candidates than thirty average ones. Every shortlist is curated, never a CV dump.

People Centred

Candidates aren't commodities and clients aren't accounts. We treat everyone with the respect a real relationship deserves.

Speed With Care

We move fast because hiring is time-sensitive — but never so fast that we compromise on the right fit.

Sector Expertise

Our consultants specialise in the industries they serve, so they speak your language and understand your roles.

Accountability

We stand behind our work with placement guarantees and ongoing support — long after the offer is signed.
